WHEREAS, City Ordinance No. 2012-037 provided the minimum requirements of the
composition and organizational structure of the Makati Disaster Risk Reduction and Management
Office (Makati DRRMO);

WHEREAS, there is a need to amend City Ordinance No. 2012-037 to consider the
recommendations and findings on the creation of the Makati DRRMO_ by the Department of
Budget Management ~ National Capital Region so as to make it more compliant to existing
policies, budgetary guidelines and circulars;

WHEREAS, there is a need to comply with the prescribed policies and guidelines for the
establishment of local Disaster Risk Reduction and Management Offices (LDRRMOs) in local
government units (LGUs) as enunciated in the Joint Memorandum Circular No. 2014-1 with the
subject “Implementing Guidelines for the Establishment of Local DRRM Offices (LDRRMOs) or
Barangay DRRM Committees (BDRRMCs) in Local Government Units (LGUs)” promulgated by
the National Disaster Risk Reduction and Management Council (NDRRMC), the Department of
the Interior and Local Government (DILG), the Department of Budget and Management (DBM)
and the Civil Service Commission (CSC).

NOW THEREFORE, BE IT ENACTED, AS IT IS HEREBY ENACTED BY THE
SANGGUNIANG PANLUNGSOD OF MAKATI, METRO MANILA, BY VIRTUE OF
THE POWERS VESTED IN IT BY LAW, IN SESSION ASSEMBLED, THAT:

Section 1. Section 4 of City Ordinance No. 2012-037 is hereby amended to read as follows:

“Section 4. Composition and Organizational Structure — The Makati DRRMO shail
be headed by the Disaster Risk Reduction and Management Officer with the following
divisions:

a) Administrative and Training Division
b) Research and Planning Division
c) Operations and Warning Division

“The amended organizational structure, which is hereto attached as Annex “A” and made
an integral part of this Ordinance, shall be composed of personnel necessary for the
performance of its mandated functions.

“Tt shall be understood that the Makati Command, Control and Communication (C3) and
Makati Rescue shall be integrated in the organizational structure of the Makati DRRMO
as deemed fit based on qualification standards and in accordance with R.A. 10121 and
other existing rules and regulations.”

Section 2. Let copy of this Ordinance be furnished to the Makati Disaster Risk Reduction and

Management Office (Makati DRRMO), Human Resource Development Office and all other
concerned departments/offices for their information and guidance.

Section 3. This ordinance shall take effect immediately upon its approval.
