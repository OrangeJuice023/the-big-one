RESOLUTION DECLARING MARIKINA CITY UNDER A STATE OF
CALAMITY DUE TO THE DEVASTATING EFFECTS OF TYPHOON
"CRISING" AND THE INTENSIFIED SOUTHWEST MONSOON
("HABAGAT"), WHICH HAS RESULTED IN WIDESPREAD FLOODING IN
LOW-LYING BARANGAYS, CAUSING DAMAGE TO PROPERTIES AND
DISRUPTING THE LIVES OF RESIDENTS

WHEREAS, the City of Marikina experienced heavy and incessant rainfall for several
days since Monday, July 21, due to the enhanced southwest monsoon (“Habagat”)
intensified by Typhoon “Crising”;

WHEREAS, | said heavy rains resulted in widespread flooding affecting numerous
barangays in Marikina City, submerging low-lying areas near the river and causing
significant damage to property and infrastructure;

WHEREAS, many areas in Marikina City remain submerged as of July 22, posing an
ongoing threat to public safety and well-being;

WHEREAS, the Marikina City Disaster Risk Reduction and Management Council
(MCDRRMC) conducted initial assessments and determined the extent of the damage
and the urgent need for immediate action to provide relief, support, and assistance to
affected residents and barangays;

WHEREAS, the extent of damage to property, infrastructure, and the continuing threat
to public safety necessitate the declaration of a state of calamity to facilitate the efficient
and effective utilization of resources for relief, rehabilitation, and recovery efforts;

WHEREAS, Section 458 (1) (iv) of Republic Act No. 7160 states that the Sangguniang
Panlungsod shall "Adopt measures to protect the inhabitants of the city from the harmful
effects of man-made or natural disasters and calamities, and to provide relief services and
assistance for victims during and in the aftermath of said disasters or calamities and their
return to productive livelihood following said events";

NOW, THEREFORE, IT IS RESOLVED AS IT IS HEREBY RESOLVED by the
SANGGUNIANG PANLUNGSOD of MARIKINA, in session duly assembled, to
declare Marikina City under the State of Calamity due to the devastating effects of
Typhoon "Crising" and the intensified southwest monsoon (Habagat), which has resulted
in widespread flooding in low-lying barangays, causing damage to properties and
disrupting the lives of the residents;

RESOLVED FURTHER, to authorize the City Mayor to utilize all available resources
of the City Government, including the quick response fund, to implement necessary
relief, rescue, rehabilitation, and recovery operations in the affected areas within the city
and coordinate with national government agencies, national government organizations,
and other stakeholders to ensure a comprehensive disaster response and facilitate
recovery efforts;

RESOLVED FURTHERMORE, to direct the MCDRRMC to continuously monitor the
situation, conduct regular assessments of the damage, and provide regular updates to the
Sangguniang Panlungsod on the progress of relief and rehabilitation efforts;

RESOLVED FINALLY, to furnish a copy of this Resolution to the Honorable City
Mayor Marjorie Ann A. Teodoro, the Honorable Secretary Juanito Victor C. Remulla of
the Department of the Interior and Local Government, other concerned national
government agencies and offices, for their information and appropriate action.

ADOPTED, by the SANGGUNIANG PANLUNGSOD of MARIKINA, this 23" day
of July, 2025.
