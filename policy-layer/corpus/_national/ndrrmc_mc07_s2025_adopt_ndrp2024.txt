SUBJECT ADOPTION OF THE NATIONAL DISASTER RESPONSE

PLAN (NDRP) 2024

1. Reference: OP Memorandum Circular no. 100 dated September 23, 2025

2. Background
The National Disaster Response Plan (NDRP) 2024, developed by the NDRRMC,

spearheaded by the Department of Social Welfare and Development (DSWD) and the
Office of Civil Defense (OCD), is the updated multi-hazard response and early
recovery plan for the Philippines, formulated through extensive stakeholder

consultations and processes.

Funded and supported by the World Bank and the Global Facility for Disaster
Reduction and Recovery (GFDRR), it embodies a collaborative and inclusive approach

to disaster resilience.

As a strategic, national-level blueprint, the NDRP 2024 aligns with existing policy
frameworks, including RA 10121, the NDRRMP 2020-2030, and the Sendai
Framework, and integrates risk-informed disaster governance with institutional

coordination across multiple sectors.

3. Objective
This Circular aims to:
a. Formaily adopt the NDRP 2024 as the standard reference for disaster response
and early recovery at all administrative levels.
b. Ensure effective dissemination, orientation, and institutional integration

nationwide.
Foster unified response operations through standardized protocols, clusters,

C
and Incident Management systems embedded within the NDRP.
