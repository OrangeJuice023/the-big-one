# Quezon City — DRRM Evidence & Legal Register (PRIMARY, public)

> Source basis: **Quezon City DRRM Plan 2021–2027, Vol. 1** (QCDRRMC, adopted via
> QCDRRMC Resolution No. 6 s.2020) and the **QCDRRMO official page**. Philippine
> government works — **not under copyright (RA 8293 §176)**; attribution required.
> Facts below are paraphrased from those primary documents. Direct URLs in
> `ledger/manifest.csv`. **All primary and publicly downloadable** — this is the
> "what full public compliance looks like" anchor (contrast the Makati OB4
> access-lapse and the Pasig FOI-gated plan).

## Headline
Quezon City evidences **8/8 obligations, all public, zero lapses.** The LDRRMP is
a downloadable PDF; hazard maps (Risk Atlas 2014) are public; the LDRRMF is
adopted annually by resolution with peso figures. QC sits at the top of the
transparency gradient — above Makati (which has an OB4 access-lapse) and Pasig
(plan exists but is FOI-gated).

## Cross-reference to the loss model (for the paper)
QC's official earthquake scenario is drawn from **MMEIRS (2004)**: the West Valley
Fault "Big One" at **M7.2**, intensity **VIII–IX**, ~**69 km** of ground rupture,
liquefaction along the WVF, and ~34,000 metro-wide deaths. This is the *same*
hazard basis our loss model uses (WVF trace + MMEIRS anchor), so QC's plan
**independently corroborates** the model's scenario. The LDRRMP also names the WVF
barangays it transects (Bagong Silangan, Batasan Hills, Matandang Balara, Pansol,
Blue Ridge B, Libis, Bagumbayan, White Plains, Ugong Norte) — useful for
per-barangay validation against our per-LGU losses.

## Legal register (DRRM instruments named in the LDRRMP)
Executive Orders:
- **EO 07 s.2010** — organized the QCDRRMC (functions, composition, roles).
- **EO 23 s.2010** — organized/redefined the QCDRRMC pursuant to RA 10121.
- **EO 71 s.2011** — created the **West Valley Fault Task Group** under QCDRRMC
  (earthquake mitigation; 5–10 m WVF buffer-zone safety). *Seismic sub-lens.*
- **EO 05 s.2013** — tasked the QCDRRMO to run an integrated all-hazards system.
- **EO 11 / 11A / 11B s.2016** — QCDRRMC reorg; forced-evacuation authority; ICS.
- **EO 04 & 07 s.2019** — reconstituted/reorganized the QCDRRMC (incl. Novaliches).

Ordinances:
- **SP-2290 s.2014** — **creates the QCDRRMO** (functions, composition, appropriating
  funds). → OB1/OB3 primary instrument.
- **SP-2424 s.2015** — amends SP-2290 (positions/salary grades per JMC 2014-1).
- **SP-3170 s.2023** — reorganizes QCDRRMO structure (+51 plantilla positions).
- **SP-2549 s.2016** — mandatory **seismic retrofitting** for QC/barangay buildings
  built before the 1990 earthquake. *Seismic sub-lens.*
- **SP-2674 s.2017** — requires establishments to run **earthquake drills** twice/yr.
- **SP-2502 s.2016** — QC Comprehensive **Zoning** Ordinance of 2016 (→ OB8).
- **SP-2688 s.2018** — institutionalizes ICS. (Others: SP-1560/2029/2112/2232/2262/2711.)

QCDRRMC / Council Resolutions (selected):
- **Res. 6 s.2020** — adopts the **QCDRRMP 2021–2027** (the OB2 artifact).
- **Res. 1 s.2019** — adopts **LDRRMF + Plan 2020 = PHP 1,180,380,939.00**.
- **Res. 2 s.2019** — Unexpended Fund 2014–2018 = PHP 1,571,690,344.82 → Special Trust Fund.
- **Res. 4 s.2017 / Res. 12 s.2018** — annual LDRRMF + QRF adoption.
- **Res. 4 s.2018 / SP-7098 s.2017 / SP-7460 s.2018** — QC **Contingency Plan for Earthquake**.
- **SP-7086 s.2017** — QC **Local Climate Change Action Plan** (→ OB8).
- **SP-6111 s.2014** — semi-annual synchronized drills (schools/malls/hospitals).

## Obligation mapping (all PRESENT, public)
- **OB1 (council institutionalized):** QCDRRMC organized EO 07/23 s.2010, reconstituted
  EO 07 s.2019.
- **OB2 (LDRRMP):** QCDRRMP 2021–2027 (public PDF; 4 thematic areas, 76 PPAs, M&E;
  adopted Res. 6 s.2020). Predecessor LDRRMP 2014–2020 built with EMI ("Building a
  Disaster Resilient QC" project).
- **OB3 (DRRMO created & staffed):** Ord. SP-2290 s.2014 (+ SP-2424 s.2015, SP-3170 s.2023).
- **OB4 (hazard maps publicly displayed):** **QC Risk Atlas (2014)** publicly downloadable
  + HVRA hazard mapping. **No access-lapse** (contrast Makati).
- **OB5 (risk assessment):** QC **HVRA completed 2013** (earthquake + flood; Urban Disaster
  Risk Index; hotspot barangays), CDRA process; MMEIRS-based EQ scenario.
- **OB6 (drills/capacity):** Ord. SP-2674 s.2017 + Res. SP-6111 s.2014 (mandatory drills);
  QCDRRMO citywide earthquake drills since 2015; Citizen's Charter services.
- **OB7 (LDRRM Fund):** ≥5% LDRRMF per RA 10121 §21; adopted annually by resolution with
  peso figures (e.g., PHP 1.18B for 2020). Hard fiscal proof.
- **OB8 (mainstreaming):** DRR/CCA mainstreamed into the CLUP via HLURB-required CDRA;
  Zoning Ord. SP-2502 s.2016; LCCAP SP-7086 s.2017; aligned to NDRRMF/NDRRMP, Sendai, SDGs.
